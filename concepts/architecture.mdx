## Hybrid Architecture Design

ACE-Step 1.5 uses a novel **hybrid architecture** combining two core components that work together to achieve commercial-grade music generation:

```
User Input → [5Hz LM] → Semantic Blueprint → [DiT] → Audio
              ↓
         Metadata Inference
         Caption Optimization
         Structure Planning
```

### Why This Architecture?

Traditional methods let diffusion models generate audio directly from text, but text-to-audio mapping is too vague and imprecise. ACE-Step introduces the Language Model as an intelligent intermediate layer:

- **LM excels at understanding semantics and planning** - transforming vague user intent into precise musical structure
- **DiT excels at generating high-fidelity audio** - crafting detailed timbre, mixing, and acoustic characteristics
- **They work together, each doing their part** - achieving both creative intelligence and audio quality

## The Planner: 5Hz Language Model

The Language Model functions as an **"omni-capable planner"** responsible for understanding your creative intent and making comprehensive musical plans.

### Chain-of-Thought Reasoning

The LM uses Chain-of-Thought (CoT) reasoning to:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Infer Music Metadata">
    Automatically determines BPM, key/scale, time signature, and duration based on your caption and lyrics.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Optimize Captions">
    Expands and refines your simple descriptions into rich, detailed prompts that guide the DiT model.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Generate Semantic Codes">
    Creates 5Hz semantic codes that implicitly contain composition melody, orchestration, and timbre information.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### LM Model Variants

The LM is available in three sizes, all trained on identical data:

| Model | Parameters | World Knowledge | Memory | Use Case |
|-------|------------|-----------------|--------|----------|
| `acestep-5Hz-lm-0.6B` | 600M | Basic | Weak | Low VRAM (6-8GB), rapid prototyping |
| `acestep-5Hz-lm-1.7B` | 1.7B | Medium | Medium | **Default recommendation** (8-16GB) |
| `acestep-5Hz-lm-4B` | 4B | Rich | Strong | Complex tasks, best quality (≥24GB) |

<Note>
  **The LM is optional.** If you're very clear about what you want, or already have reference audio, you can skip the LM planning step entirely by disabling `thinking` mode. In Cover or Repaint modes, **you replace the LM's work** - becoming the planner yourself.
</Note>

### What LM Learns

The LM learns **world knowledge** from training data:

- Understanding of 1000+ instruments and musical styles
- Recognition of emotional and atmospheric descriptors
- Knowledge of song structures and progressions
- Multi-language lyric generation (50+ languages)
- Audio understanding capabilities (BPM, key detection, captioning)

## The Executor: Diffusion Transformer (DiT)

The DiT model is the **"audio craftsman"** responsible for turning semantic plans into reality through the diffusion process.

### How DiT Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive Input">
    Accepts semantic codes and conditions generated by the LM, plus optional reference audio embeddings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Diffusion Process">
    Gradually "carves" high-fidelity audio from pure noise through iterative denoising steps.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Audio Generation">
    Decides final timbre, mixing balance, stereo imaging, and all acoustic details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### DiT Model Variants

ACE-Step 1.5 provides multiple DiT models optimized for different use cases:

#### Turbo Series (Recommended)

Distilled for ultra-fast generation in just **8 steps**:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="turbo (default)" icon="bolt">
    Joint distillation on shift 1, 2, 3. Best balance of creativity and semantic adherence. Thoroughly tested.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="turbo-shift1" icon="palette">
    Distilled only on shift=1. Richer details and texture, but weaker semantic adherence.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="turbo-shift3" icon="bullseye">
    Distilled only on shift=3. Clearer timbre and stronger semantics, but may sound "dry" with minimal orchestration.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="turbo-continuous" icon="sliders">
    Experimental. Supports continuous shift 1-5 for maximum tuning flexibility.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  **Understanding Shift**: The shift hyperparameter determines attention allocation during denoising:
  - **Higher shift** → More effort on early denoising (building structure), stronger semantics
  - **Lower shift** → More even distribution, more details (but details might also be noise)
</Warning>

#### SFT Model

Supervised Fine-Tuned model with 50 steps:

- ✅ Supports CFG (Classifier-Free Guidance) for fine-tuning prompt adherence
- ✅ More denoising steps for richer detail expression
- ❌ Slower inference (50 steps vs 8 for Turbo)
- ❌ More error accumulation, slightly less crisp than Turbo

#### Base Model

**Master of all tasks** with exclusive capabilities:

| Task | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `extract` | Separate single tracks from mixed audio (e.g., isolate vocals) |
| `lego` | Add new tracks to existing audio (e.g., add drums to guitar) |
| `complete` | Generate full mixed accompaniment for single track (e.g., add backing to vocals) |

<Note>
  The Base model also has the **strongest fine-tuning plasticity**. If you have large-scale training needs, start with Base to create your own custom SFT model.
</Note>

## VAE Encoder/Decoder

The Variational Autoencoder serves as the bridge between audio waveforms and the latent space where DiT operates.

### VAE Functions

<Accordion title="Encoding Audio to Latents">
  Compresses stereo 48kHz audio into compact latent representations that preserve musical structure and acoustic features while reducing computational requirements.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Decoding Latents to Audio">
  Reconstructs high-fidelity audio from latent codes generated by DiT. Uses adaptive tiling strategies to handle various VRAM constraints.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Reference Audio Processing">
  Encodes reference audio into acoustic feature embeddings that guide DiT generation for timbre and style transfer.
</Accordion>

### Memory-Adaptive Decoding

The VAE decoder automatically adapts to your GPU's available VRAM:

```python
GPU tiled decode → GPU decode with CPU offload → Full CPU decode
```

Chunk sizes automatically adjust: 64/128/256/512/1024/1536 based on free memory.

## Component Integration

Here's how the components work together for different generation modes:

### Text-to-Music Generation

1. **User provides**: Caption, lyrics, optional metadata
2. **LM processes**: Generates semantic codes, infers metadata via CoT
3. **DiT receives**: Semantic codes + conditions
4. **DiT generates**: Latent audio through diffusion
5. **VAE decodes**: Latents → 48kHz stereo audio

### Cover Generation

1. **User provides**: Source audio + new caption/lyrics
2. **VAE encodes**: Source audio → semantic structure codes
3. **DiT receives**: Structural codes + new conditions
4. **DiT generates**: New interpretation maintaining structure
5. **VAE decodes**: Latents → remixed audio

<Note>
  In Cover mode, the LM can be bypassed entirely - the source audio structure replaces the LM's compositional planning.
</Note>

### Repaint/Local Editing

1. **User provides**: Source audio + time interval + modifications
2. **VAE encodes**: Source audio for context
3. **DiT processes**: Context-aware local modification
4. **DiT generates**: Modified segment that blends seamlessly
5. **VAE decodes**: Complete audio with local changes

## Technical Innovations

### Intrinsic Reinforcement Learning

Uniquely, the alignment between LM and DiT is achieved through **intrinsic reinforcement learning** relying solely on the model's internal mechanisms, eliminating biases from external reward models or human preferences.

### Unified Architecture

Beyond standard synthesis, ACE-Step 1.5 unifies:

- Precise stylistic control
- Versatile editing capabilities (cover, repaint, vocal-to-BGM)
- Strict prompt adherence across languages
- High-speed inference
- Local deployment and ownership

This makes it a powerful tool that seamlessly integrates into creative workflows of music artists, producers, and content creators.
